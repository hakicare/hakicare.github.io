About Our Platform

Our care management platform is specially designed & developed for eldercare organizations to assist them in providing world-class care & scaling their organizations effortlessly. It is a one-stop solution for managing your day-to-day care delivery requirements at your fingertips.

We are a group from IIT Bombay and built this platform in collaboration with a leading eldercare organization, taking into account the specific needs & requirements of different stakeholders like Caregivers, doctors & Administration.
