Our Mission

We at Haki Care strive to provide quality assistance to elderly. We understand the challenges families face when loved ones are affected and we plan on helping families through our homecare services and innovative products. We serve to seniors and families requiring dementia care and post operation nursing at home.



All-Round Care

A personal health manager alloted to family to understand the needs and problems better, will help you with everything along the journey. Regular scheduled checkups with relevant doctors and implementing the feedback into nursing for maximum benefits.

F.A.Q

  • What are the early-stage symptoms of dementia?

    The early stage of dementia is often overlooked because the onset is gradual. Common symptoms include:

    forgetfulness
    losing track of the time
    becoming lost in familiar places.

  • What is the treatment available for dementia?

    There is no treatment currently available to cure dementia or to alter its progressive course. However, much can be offered to support and improve the lives of people with dementia and their carers and families. The principal goals for dementia care are:

    optimizing physical health, cognition, activity and well-being
    providing information and long-term support to carers.

  • Why do I need a dementia care specialist?

    Dementia is a complex disease that involves medical, social and psychological issues that may not be entirely addressed by primary care physicians and specialists. Haki Care program fills the gaps in care for dementia patients and their families
